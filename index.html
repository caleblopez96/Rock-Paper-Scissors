Rock, Paper, Scissors!

Rock, Paper, Scissors, also known as Rochambeau or Roshambo is a game of chance that depends on random luck similar to flipping coins.

The game is simple:

  • Rock beats scissors
  • Paper beats rock
  • Scissors beats paper
  • * If both players make the same move, it is considered a tie, and play resumes until there is a clear winner *
